Existing EntityExtractor in `src-tauri/src/pipeline/entities.rs` uses `extract_with_ner(text, ner_closure)` — Phase 6 pattern where NER is passed as a closure. Consequence: the extract_with_ner API can either (a) stay (Phase 6 backward compat — the closure just returns empty) or (b) be simplified to a `regex_only_extract` method. Pick (b) as a modest cleanup; the closure was carrying dead weight after NER gating.
